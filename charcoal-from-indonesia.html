The Benefits of Charcoal From Indonesia

Charcoal from Indonesia - Indonesia country's natural wealth is actually very rich, especially the product of coconut commodity which is the main raw material of coconut shell charcoal briquettes. Did you know? Indonesia country is the first in coconut productivity in the Asian region?

Based on research, briquettes made from coconut shell charcoal have many advantages over briquettes made from charcoal or other fuels. See the following description.

  • Renewable energy

    • So what I mean by renewable energy is energy that we can get and come from natural sources, now this raw material is the result of a stable natural process. It will never run out because it is always produced by nature as long as we continue to preserve it.

  • Biomass

    • Coconut charcoal briquettes are included in the energy of biomass category. Biomass is a type of renewable energy that refers to biological material derived from 100% natural material. Some examples of biophysical sources include firewood, coconut, garbage, and fossil-energy.

  • Maximum heat generated

    • In contrast to charcoal briquettes, it turns out that the level of heat produced by coconut shell charcoal briquettes is quite high at 7000 cal. Therefore, high-calorie ingredients allow our cooking process to cook faster and more perfectly.

  • Not toxic

    • Now, the process of making coconut charcoal briquettes is naturally and eco-friendly, the product of briquettes are safe for us. The only additive material is a kind of tapioca starch obtained from cassava as a briquette dough adhesive. It makes coconut charcoal briquettes a safe and non-toxic material.

  • no smoke

    • If you burn charcoal it will usually emit a lot of smoke, for example, you can see when you make a chicken barbeque with wood charcoal a lot of smoke will come out, even if the chicken barbeque smells fragrant. vBut it is a different thing if you use this coconut charcoal briquette. When it burns, very little smoke is produced, almost not smoky, so it does not disturb your neighbor.

  • Switch to green energy

    • The most famous benefits of using coconut shell charcoal briquettes. Go Green Energy is the term for an environmentally friendly energy source.

    Yes, as I said earlier, with the advantages of coconut charcoal briquettes made from raw coconut and cassava flour additives, if baked smoke-free, then these coconut shell charcoal briquettes are very environmentally friendly.

  • Charcoal substitute

    • If we look at it often, the fuel used mainly for the industry is coal. But from time to time, based on some studies, it turns out that the discovery of coal on a large scale will be very dangerous to nature.

    In addition, it turns out that charcoal, more likely and classified as toxic, is inhaled by humans. This is why coconut shell charcoal briquettes can be an alternative fuel solution for charcoal.

  • Long burning time

    • Coconut Charcoal Briquettes have a long burning time, about 2-3 hours non-stop. This makes it a relatively efficient and effective fuel.

  • As an alternative to the use of gas stoves

    • Almost all of us use gas stoves for cooking, right? Moreover, now that gas prices continue to rise and sometimes consumption is still low, we are disappointed.

Therefore, in line with the development of technology and the times, some manufacturers of coconut shell charcoal from Indonesia have seen their potential in making briquette stoves. The objective is to replace the gas stoves used by households so far.
